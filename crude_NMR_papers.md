# Papers: Crude ¹H NMR Spectra from Reactions

Real experimental spectra from reaction mixtures (paper or SI). Excludes [acs.jcim.3c01864](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/full/10.1021/acs.jcim.3c01864).

---

## Confirmed: Crude Spectra Figures in SI

**1. Halide Inhibition of CuAAC** — Moorman et al., *Org. Biomol. Chem.* 2015  
~15 crude ¹H NMR spectra (400 MHz, CDCl₃) at varying halide loadings. Integration of CH₂CO₂Et peaks for azide→triazole conversion.  
[Paper (RSC)](https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2015/ob/c4ob02479f) · [SI PDF](https://www.rsc.org/suppdata/ob/c4/c4ob02479f/c4ob02479f1.pdf)

**2. Selective NMR for Crude Mixture Analysis** — Dal Poggetto et al., *Anal. Chem.* 2020, 92, 14047  
Crude ¹H NMR of α-fluorination and NaBH₄ reduction; 92–209 mg samples unpurified. Raw NMR data open.  
[Paper (ACS)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.analchem.0c02976) · [Raw NMR data (Zenodo)](https://doi.org/10.25824/redu/LNVQT9)

**3. Doping Experiments in ¹H NMR Mixture Analysis** — Kaicharla, Chinta & Hoye, *J. Org. Chem.* 2022, 87, 5007  
Crude NMR of butanoic acid pyrolysate (600 °C); annotated figures for 13 components across orders of magnitude.  
[Paper (PMC)](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9583569/)

## Confirmed: Crude NMR Central to Analysis

**4. NMR Reaction Monitoring Robust to Spectral Distortions** — Domzal et al., *Anal. Chem.* 2025  
Raw NMR from sucrose hydrolysis, pentene and hexene hydrosilylation. Open dataset indexed by time.  
[Paper (PMC)](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12311900/) · [Dataset (Zenodo)](https://zenodo.org/records/14814657)

**5. Clean ¹H NMR Directly from Batch and Flow Mixtures** — Horbenko et al., *ChemistryEurope* 2025  
Crude NMR from click reactions; DOSY-based separation. Full spectral dataset open.  
[Paper (Wiley)](https://chemistry-europe.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/ceur.202500038) · [Dataset (Zenodo)](https://zenodo.org/records/14281508)

**6. In Situ NMR Monitoring of Cyclopropanation** — Porta et al., *Org. Process Res. Dev.* 2016, 20, 1603  
Crude flow reaction spectra from hypervalent iodine(III) cyclopropanation; includes J-RES 2D of crude mixtures.  
[Paper (ACS, CC-BY)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.oprd.6b00177)

**7. Real-Time Monitoring of CuAAC/SPAAC via Benchtop NMR** — *ACS Meas. Sci. Au* 2023  
Crude NMR arrays over time at 60 MHz; SABRE hyperpolarization for low-concentration signals.  
[Paper (PMC)](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10120034/)

**8. Chemoenzymatic One-Pot Synthesis of α-Hydroxy Half-Esters** — *ACS Omega* 2021  
Conversion and dr by ¹H NMR of crude mixtures from [2,3]-Wittig / PLE hydrolysis cascade.  
[Paper (PMC)](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC8359131/)

**9. ¹³C NMR for Quantitative Isomer Determination** — Hoye et al., *Org. Lett.* 2014, 16, 1448  
Crude ¹H and ¹³C NMR integration for dr; demonstrates overlap cases where ¹³C is preferred.  
[Paper (ACS)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/ol403776k)

**10. Organocatalytic Michael/Acyl Transfer — dr from Crude ¹H NMR** — Parida & Pan, *Beilstein J. Org. Chem.* 2021  
dr from integration at 5.21 ppm (cis) / 5.15 ppm (trans). Open SI available.  
[Paper (Beilstein)](https://www.beilstein-journals.org/bjoc/articles/17/100) · [SI PDF](https://www.beilstein-journals.org/bjoc/content/supplementary/1860-5397-17-100-S1.pdf)

**11. Modular Stereodivergent Synthesis — dr from Crude ¹H NMR** — *Org. Lett.* 2017  
Crude NMR as primary analytical method across full substrate scope before isolation.  
[Paper (ACS)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.orglett.6b03841)

**12. Microwave-Assisted Ring-Closing Metathesis** — Vanier et al., *J. Org. Chem.* 2003, 68, 8347  
Conversion of Grubbs RCM on 6 dienes by crude ¹H NMR; conventional vs. microwave comparison.  
[Paper (ACS)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/jo035135c)

**13. Total Synthesis of Ulodione Natural Products** — Lawrence et al., *Angew. Chem.* 2022 (CC-BY-NC-ND)  
Crude NMR detects minor product ±ulodione C at 7% (1:8 ratio); key to structural revision.  
[Paper (Wiley)](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/anie.202207004)

**14. Flow Reactors with Inline Benchtop NMR (Review)** — Felpin et al., *Chem. Soc. Rev.*  
Crude ¹H NMR from flow reactions (CEES oxidation, cyclopropanation, Menschutkin); spectra every 13 s at 43.62 MHz.  
[Open PDF (HAL)](https://hal.science/hal-02140934/file/Felpin-Manuscript-15062018.pdf)

**15. Buchwald–Hartwig Amination — NMR Yield from Crude** — *Green Chem.* 2022  
Crude ¹H NMR with TMB internal standard for NMR yield of C–N coupling products.  
[SI PDF (RSC)](https://www.rsc.org/suppdata/d2/gc/d2gc01460b/d2gc01460b2.pdf)

**16. C–H Functionalization with Unactivated Alkyl Bromides** — *J. Org. Chem.* 2018  
NMR yield from crude mixture with 1,4-dinitrobenzene internal standard; crude spectra in SI.  
[Paper (ACS)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.joc.8b01589)
